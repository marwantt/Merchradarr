# Amazon Merch Bullet Points Guidelines

Writing effective bullet points for Amazon Merch listings is crucial for both compliance and conversions. Amazon has specific guidelines to ensure all content is appropriate and beneficial for both buyers and sellers. In this guide, we will explore Amazon's content policy, what the algorithm favors, how to utilize the two-bullet format, and offer a template for crafting compliant, high-converting bullet points.

## Amazon's Content Policy for Merch Listings

When creating bullet points for your Merch by Amazon listings, you must adhere to several content policies:

- **No Health Claims**: Avoid making any health-related claims unless they are substantiated and allowed by Amazon's policy.
- **No Offensive Content**: Content must be free from hate speech, profanity, or anything deemed offensive.
- **No External URLs**: Do not include links to external websites or platforms.
- **No Pricing Information**: Do not mention pricing, discounts, or any price-related terms.
- **No Competitor Mentions**: Avoid mentioning competitors directly or indirectly.

### What Amazon's Algorithm Looks for in Bullets

Amazon's algorithm prioritizes:

- **Relevance**: Bullet points should be relevant to the product and contain pertinent information.
- **Clarity**: Information should be clear and concise, easily understandable by the customer.
- **Keywords**: Naturally integrate keywords to help the product appear in relevant searches.
- **Compliance**: Adherence to Amazon's guidelines is crucial to avoid listing suspension.

## The 2-Bullet Format

Amazon provides a two-bullet format for Merch listings. Each bullet serves a specific purpose:

### Bullet 1: Key Product Features

Focus on the primary features of the product:

- Describe the design and materials used.
- Highlight any unique aspects that set the product apart.
- Use this space to engage the customer with compelling, factual information.

### Bullet 2: Customer Benefits and Use Cases

Emphasize how the product benefits the customer:

- Explain the practical uses or benefits of the product.
- Include lifestyle or situational applications that resonate with potential buyers.
- Avoid overloading with features; focus on the customer's perspective.

## Prohibited Words and Phrases

To maintain compliance, avoid the following:

- **"Best"**: Avoid subjective superlatives unless substantiated.
- **"Guarantee"**: Do not promise outcomes or results.
- **"Free"**: Do not use unless part of an Amazon-approved promotion.
- **"Cure" or "Prevent"**: Avoid unsubstantiated health claims.

## Examples of Rejected vs. Approved Bullet Points

### Rejected Bullet Point

- **"Our t-shirt is the best on the market! It's totally free of any harmful substances and will cure your discomfort!"**

### Approved Bullet Point

- **"Crafted from 100% breathable cotton, this t-shirt offers comfort and style perfect for everyday wear."**

## How to Naturally Include Keywords

Incorporate keywords without stuffing:

- Identify 2-3 primary keywords relevant to your product.
- Use synonyms or related terms to maintain natural language flow.
- Integrate keywords into sentences where they fit organically, rather than forcing them.

### Example

Instead of: **"Funny cat t-shirt, cat lover gift, cat t-shirt"**

Use: **"This funny cat t-shirt makes a purr-fect gift for any cat lover, offering a playful design for casual outings."**

## Template for Writing Compliant, High-Converting Bullets

Below is a template to guide your bullet creation process:

### Bullet 1: Key Product Features

- **"Crafted from [material], this [product type] features [unique design aspect], ensuring [specific benefit]."**

### Bullet 2: Customer Benefits and Use Cases

- **"Ideal for [specific occasion or use], this [product type] provides [benefit or feature], making it perfect for [target audience]."**

### Example Using Template

**Bullet 1**: "Crafted from high-quality, eco-friendly cotton, this graphic tee features a unique hand-drawn design, ensuring a stylish and sustainable choice."

**Bullet 2**: "Ideal for casual outings or relaxed weekends, this tee offers all-day comfort and a fun design, perfect for eco-conscious fashion enthusiasts."

By following these guidelines and using the provided template, you can create effective, compliant bullet points that enhance your product's visibility and appeal on Amazon Merch. Remember, clarity, compliance, and customer focus are key to writing successful bullet points.
